📥 How to Download
  1. Tap View & Download Photos above to open the album
  2. Tap any photo to open it full size
  3. Tap the three-dot menu (or the download icon on desktop) and choose Download
  4. Photos save to your phone's photo library or your computer's Downloads folder
📤 How to Share Yours
  1. Tap Share Your Photos above to open the album
  2. On mobile, tap the + (New) button → Upload
  3. On desktop, just drag and drop your photos anywhere on the album page
  4. Pick photos from your phone library or computer, confirm, and they upload automatically
  5. Everyone with the album link can see and download them. Be considerate of others when sharing photos with them in it.
